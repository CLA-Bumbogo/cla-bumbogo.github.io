About Our Branch

Christian Life Assembly — Bumbogo is a local branch of the wider CLA family in Rwanda. We are a Christ-centered community committed to worship, discipleship, and serving our neighbourhood.

Whether you are visiting for the first time or have been part of the family for years, there is a place for you here. Join us on Sunday, or explore our sermons and photo galleries any time.
